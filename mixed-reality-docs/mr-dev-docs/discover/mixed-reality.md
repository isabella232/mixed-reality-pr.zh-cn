# <a name="what-is-mixed-reality"></a><span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-104">什么是混合现实？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-104">What is Mixed Reality?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-105">混合现实是计算领域的下一波浪潮，紧随其后的是大型机、电脑和智能手机。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-105">Mixed reality is the next wave in computing followed by mainframes, PCs, and smartphones.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-106">混合现实正在消费者和企业之间成为主流。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-106">Mixed reality is going mainstream for consumers and businesses.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-107">它提供与居住空间数据、事务以及好友之间的本能交互，将我们从受屏幕束缚的体验中解放出来。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-107">It liberates us from screen-bound experiences, by offering instinctual interactions with data in our living spaces, among our things, and with our friends.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-108">世界各地数以亿计的在线探索者通过他们的手持设备体验了混合现实。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-108">Online explorers, in hundreds of millions around the world, have experienced mixed reality through their handheld devices.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-109">移动 AR 提供了目前社交媒体上最主流的混合现实解决方案。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-109">Mobile AR offers the most mainstream mixed reality solutions today on social media.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-110">人们甚至可能没有意识到，他们在 Instagram 上使用的 AR 滤镜就是混合现实体验。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-110">People may not even realize that the AR filters they use on Instagram are Mixed Reality experiences.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-111">Microsoft 混合现实将真正令人惊叹的人体全息影像、高保真全息 3D 模型及周围的现实世界结合起来，将所有这些用户体验提升到新的水平。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-111">Microsoft Mixed Reality is to take all these user experiences to the next level with a combination of truly stunning holographic representations of people, high fidelity holographic 3D models and the real world around them.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-113">混合现实是物理世界和数字世界的混合，开启了人、计算机和环境之间的自然且直观的 3D 交互。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-113">Mixed Reality is a blend of physical and digital worlds, unlocking natural and intuitive 3D human, computer, and environment interactions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-114">这种新的现实基于计算机视觉、图形处理、显示技术、输入系统和云计算的进步。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-114">This new reality is based on advancements in computer vision, graphical processing, display technologies, input systems, and cloud computing.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-115">Paul Milgram 和 Fumio Kishino 在其 1994 年发表的论文“[A Taxonomy of Mixed Reality Visual Displays](https://search.ieice.org/bin/summary.php?id=e77-d_12_1321)”（混合现实视频显示的分类法）中首次引入“混合现实”一词。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-115">The term Mixed Reality was introduced in a 1994 paper by Paul Milgram and Fumio Kishino, "[A Taxonomy of Mixed Reality Visual Displays](https://search.ieice.org/bin/summary.php?id=e77-d_12_1321)."</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-116">该论文中探讨了“虚拟连续体”的概念以及视觉显示的分类法。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-116">Their paper explored the concept of a *virtuality continuum* and the taxonomy of visual displays.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-117">从那此后，混合现实的应用包括以下各项，已经超越了显示内容：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-117">Since then, the application of Mixed Reality has gone beyond displays to include:</span></span>

## <a name="environmental-input-and-perception"></a><span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-125">环境输入和感知</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-125">Environmental input and perception</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-126">在过去几十年里，人机关系持续通过输入方式变化得到发展。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-126">In the past decades, the relationship between humans and computers has continued to evolve by the means of input methods.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-127">进而诞生了称作“人机交互”(HCI) 的新学科。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-127">A new discipline emerged, which is known as human-computer interaction or HCI.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-128">人类输入现可包括键盘、鼠标、触摸、笔迹、语音和 Kinect 骨骼跟踪。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-128">Human input can now include keyboards, mice, touch, ink, voice, and Kinect skeletal tracking.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-129">传感器和处理能力的进步在于基于先进的输入方法创造出了计算机对环境的新感知。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-129">Advancements in sensors and processing power are creating new computer perceptions of environments based on advanced input methods.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-130">这就是 Windows 中显示环境信息的 API 称作[感知 API](/uwp/api/Windows.Perception) 的原因。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-130">This is why API names in Windows that reveal environmental information are called the [perception APIs](/uwp/api/Windows.Perception).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-131">环境输入可以捕获：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-131">Environmental inputs can capture:</span></span> 

* <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-132">某个人的身体在物理世界中的位置（[头部跟踪](../design/coordinate-systems.md)）</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-132">a person's body position in the physical world ([head tracking](../design/coordinate-systems.md))</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-135">对象识别</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-135">object recognition</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-136">物理位置</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-136">physical locations</span></span>

## <a name="the-mixed-reality-spectrum"></a><span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-146">混合现实范围</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-146">The Mixed Reality spectrum</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-147">混合现实融合了物理世界和数字世界。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-147">Mixed Reality blends both physical and digital worlds.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-148">这两种现实定义了称作“虚拟连续体”的范围的两个极端。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-148">These two realities mark the polar ends of a spectrum known as the *virtuality continuum*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-149">我们将这一系列的现实称作“混合现实范围”。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-149">We refer to this spectrum of realities as the *Mixed Reality spectrum*.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-150">一端是人类所在的物理现实。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-150">On the end of the spectrum, we have the physical reality that we as humans exist.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-151">另一端是相对应的数字现实。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-151">On the other end of the spectrum, we have the corresponding digital reality.</span></span>

### <a name="augmented-vs-virtual-reality"></a><span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-152">增强现实与虚拟现实</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-152">Augmented vs. virtual reality</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-153">目前市场上的大多数手机几乎都不具备环境感知功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-153">Most mobile phones on the market today have little to no environmental perception capabilities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-154">这些手机提供的体验无法混合物理现实和数字现实。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-154">The experiences these phones offer can't mix physical and digital realities.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-155">在物理世界中叠加图形、视频流或全息影像的体验称为“增强现实”。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-155">The experiences that overlay graphics, video streams, or holograms in the physical world are called augmented reality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-156">遮挡视线以呈现全沉浸式数字体验的体验是“虚拟现实”。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-156">The experiences that occlude your view to present a fully immersive digital experience are virtual reality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-157">在增强现实和虚拟现实之间实现的体验形成了“混合现实”，通过它可以：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-157">The experiences that can transition between augmented and virtual realities form Mixed Reality, where you can:</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-158">在物理世界中放置一个数字对象（如全息影像），就如同它真实存在一样。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-158">Place a digital object, such as a hologram, in the physical world, as if it were physically present.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-159">在物理世界中以个人的数字形式（虚拟形象）出现，以在不同的时间点与他人异步协作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-159">Be personally and digitally present in the physical world, in the form of an avatar, to asynchronously collaborate with others at different points in time.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="9a0fe-160">在虚拟现实中，物理边界（如墙壁和家具）以数字形式出现在体验中，帮助用户避开物理障碍物。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a0fe-160">In virtual reality, physical boundaries like walls and furniture appear digitally within the experience for users to avoid running into physical obstacles.</span></span>
